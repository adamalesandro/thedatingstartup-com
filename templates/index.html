What is TheDatingStartup exactly?

“What if I ran my dating life like a startup?”

At first, it seems outlandish, maybe even absurd. But when I actually started to kick the idea around, absurdity turned to intrigue. What if I put the same effort and careful planning into my dating life that I put every day into running our company? Well then, it stands to reason that I can improve it with the same primary tools and strategy. Read More ...

Multi-Channel

Instead of pursuing a single approach, we intend to allocate efforts to a variety of mediums until there is enough data to determine the most effective.

Feedback

Our operating model entails developing a specific action plan for each aspect of dating, then refining it using rigorous testing and feedback.



Data

It is not exactly 'big data', but can we improve the process by collecting and analyzing data and to make better informed dating decisions? Yes we can.

Transparency

We will protect individual privacy, but be transparent about all normal metrics, such as CAC, Burn Rate, and Churn as well as few new ones.
